Frequently Asked Questions

What courses does Dr. Birmohan Singh teach?

Dr. Singh teaches various courses including Artificial Intelligence, Data Mining, Software Engineering, and Advanced Algorithms at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels.

How can I schedule a meeting with Dr. Singh?

You can schedule a meeting with Dr. Singh by clicking the "Book Now" button on his profile page or by contacting the CSE department office during working hours.

What research opportunities are available with Dr. Singh?

Dr. Singh offers research opportunities in areas such as Big Data Analytics, IoT, and Cloud Computing. Interested students can discuss potential projects during his office hours or via email.

Does Dr. Singh provide mentorship for industry projects?

Yes, Dr. Singh actively mentors students for industry projects, especially in the areas of AI and Software Engineering. He has strong industry connections and can provide valuable guidance for real-world applications.

What are Dr. Singh's office hours?

Dr. Singh's office hours are typically Monday to Friday, 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM. However, it's recommended to check his current schedule or make an appointment via email for the most up-to-date information.
